# trackpad

**触摸板**(**TouchPad**或**TrackPad**),是一种在[笔记本电脑](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Laptop.html)上利用感应用户手指的移动来控制[光标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Cursor.html)动作的设备。下图椭圆框出来的部分就是[触控板](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=113363468&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=触控板&zd_token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJpc3MiOiJ6aGlkYV9zZXJ2ZXIiLCJleHAiOjE3NDM5NDYxODUsInEiOiLop6bmjqfmnb8iLCJ6aGlkYV9zb3VyY2UiOiJlbnRpdHkiLCJjb250ZW50X2lkIjoxMTMzNjM0NjgsImNvbnRlbnRfdHlwZSI6IkFydGljbGUiLCJtYXRjaF9vcmRlciI6MSwiemRfdG9rZW4iOm51bGx9.Yo0v1IIdKlTJejfDQ94C9cMzXJli4wuQh-5Mn3gi8pw&zhida_source=entity),一般其上会设置两个按钮,左下边相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)左键,而右下边则是[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)右键。



- 单指操作:

- - 单指双击触摸板,相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)左键单击。
  - 单指双击触控板,相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)左键双击。

- 双指操作:

- - 单击触摸板,相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)右键,弹出[快捷菜单](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut-Menu.html)。

  - 双指同时上滑,实现屏幕向下滚动(上下方向滚动条存在时)或者切换到下一张图片,相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)滚轮向后滚动。
    双指同时下滑,实现屏幕向上滚动(上下方向滚动条存在时)或者切换到上一张图片,相当于[鼠标](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Mouse.html)滚轮向前滚动。

  - 双指同时左滑,实现屏幕向右滑动(左右方向滚动条存在时)或者切换到下一张图片,相当于[Shift+鼠标滚轮](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shift-Mouse-Pulley.html)向后滑动。
    双指同时右滑,实现屏幕向左滑动(上下方向滚动条存在时)或者切换到上一张图片,相当于[Shift+鼠标滚轮](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shift-Mouse-Pulley.html)向前滑动。

  - 双指同时向对角划开,实现放大当前的图片/页面

    双指同时向对角缩合,实现缩小当前的图片/页面
  
- 三指操作(对[VMware虚拟机](https://zhida.zhihu.com/search?content_id=113363468&content_type=Article&match_order=1&q=VMware虚拟机&zd_token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJpc3MiOiJ6aGlkYV9zZXJ2ZXIiLCJleHAiOjE3NDM5NDYxODUsInEiOiJWTXdhcmXomZrmi5_mnLoiLCJ6aGlkYV9zb3VyY2UiOiJlbnRpdHkiLCJjb250ZW50X2lkIjoxMTMzNjM0NjgsImNvbnRlbnRfdHlwZSI6IkFydGljbGUiLCJtYXRjaF9vcmRlciI6MSwiemRfdG9rZW4iOm51bGx9.qcC7nYdEHWh5Ie-NyjbXLzXO8Z4yz7jyRp-g5b5h254&zhida_source=entity)无效):

- - 单击触摸板,弹出[搜索框](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Search.html),相当于[快捷键](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut.html)[Win+S](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Win-S.html)。
  - 三指同时上滑,弹出[任务视图](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Task-View.html),相当于[快捷键](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut.html)[Win+Tab](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Win-Tab.html)。
  - 三指同时下滑,将当前所有[窗口](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Window.html)[最小化](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Minimize.html),显示[桌面](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Desktop.html),相当于快捷键[Win+D](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Win-D.html)或[Win+M](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Win-M.html)。
  - 三指同时左滑,实现快速向后切换任务程序,相当于[快捷键](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut.html)[Alt+Shift+Tab](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Alt-Shift-Tab.html)。
  - 三指同时右滑,实现快速向前切换任务程序,相当于[快捷键](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut.html)[Alt+Tab](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Alt-Tab.html)。
  
- 四指操作(对VMware虚拟机无效):

- - 四指单击,打开[操作中心](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Operation-Center.html),可以进行消息查看和开启系统功能,相当于[快捷键](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Shortcut.html)[Win+A](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Win-A.html)。
  - 四指同时向左/右划,实现在[虚拟桌面](https://link.zhihu.com/?target=https%3A//xiaoheidiannao.com/articles/Virtual-Desktop.html)间切换
